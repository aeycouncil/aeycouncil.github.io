Join the AEYC!

Are you a high school student passionate about the environment? Do you want to help promote conservation and sustainability in Alabama? Are you interested in educating the public about environmental issues, advocating for environmental policy, and becoming a leader in the statewide environmental movement? If so, we highly encourage you to apply for the 2021-2022 term of the AEYC. Qualifications, responsibilities, and more information about the application process can be found on the Application Form, due by May 7th.
